This Journal is for that. Short, honest pieces about:

- What I see on the table over and over
- What actually helps (and what doesn't, despite what your Instagram feed says)
- Self-care that holds up between sessions
- The mind-body conversation, when it's worth having

No fluff, no lectures. Just notes from someone who's been at this a while.
