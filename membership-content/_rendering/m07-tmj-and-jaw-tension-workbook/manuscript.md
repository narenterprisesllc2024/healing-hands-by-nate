# TMJ and Jaw Tension — Companion Workbook

*A printable companion to the Month 7 module. Fill it in as you go.*

---

## How to use this workbook

Print it. Keep it somewhere you'll see it — bathroom mirror, desk, kitchen counter. Fill it in over the next 7 days.

The whole point is to make conscious what's currently unconscious. You can't release jaw tension you don't know you're holding. This workbook trains you to notice.

Bring it to your next session.

## Try this for 7 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**Three jaw check-ins per day. 30 seconds each. Morning, midday, evening. Just notice if your teeth are touching, and if they are, drop the jaw. Tongue to roof of mouth. Three breaths.**

That's the whole assignment. Don't add the releases. Don't add the mobility. Just notice and reset. Three times a day for one week.

Most people are stunned by how often they catch themselves clenched once they actually look. That awareness is half the fix.

Bring this workbook to your next session. Show me what you noticed.
