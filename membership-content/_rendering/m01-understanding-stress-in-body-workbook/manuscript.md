# Understanding Stress in the Body — Companion Workbook

*A printable companion to the Month 1 module. Map your body's stress signature.*

---

## How to use this workbook

Print it. Keep it where you'll see it twice a day — bathroom counter, desk, kitchen.

This is an awareness workbook, not a fix workbook. You're collecting data on how your own body holds stress. That data is the foundation for everything that comes next.

Bring it to your next session.

---

## Where You Are Today

Do a 60-second body scan right now. Move attention through each station. Mark what you find.

**Right now, are you holding tension in:**

- [ ] Jaw (teeth touching, tongue pressed, masseter tight)
- [ ] Forehead (furrowing, behind the eyes)
- [ ] Neck (chin tucked, stiff)
- [ ] Shoulders (up toward ears, hunched forward)
- [ ] Chest (shallow breath, holding breath)
- [ ] Hands (fists clenched, fingers tight)
- [ ] Stomach (tight belly)

## Try this for 7 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

Don't worry about fixing anything. Don't worry about morning and afternoon both. Just one scan, once a day, every day.

The awareness alone is the work. Most people are stunned by how often they catch themselves bracing without having any idea.
