# Nervous System Self-Assessment — Companion Workbook

*A year-long companion to the IC4 deep cut. Build the literacy to read your own nervous system over time.*

---

## How to use this workbook

This is a long-haul workbook. Use the daily check-ins to establish baseline (first 2 weeks). Use the weekly assessment for ongoing tracking. Use the seasonal check-ins quarterly.

Bring it to every session — and consider sharing relevant pages with your therapist if you have one.

---

## Establishing Your Baseline (First 2 Weeks)

For 14 days, do the 5-question check-in three times a day. Just observe — don't try to change anything yet.

### Five-Question Check-In Template

Use this format:

```
TIME: ___________
1. Breathing: ____________
2. Heart rate: ____________
3. Hands (temperature/tension): ____________
```

## When to Seek Professional Mental Health Support

Check any that apply over the past month:

- [ ] Persistent depression or hopelessness lasting more than 2-3 weeks
- [ ] Anxiety significantly impairing daily function
- [ ] Trauma symptoms (intrusive memories, hypervigilance, flashbacks, avoidance) interfering with life
- [ ] Suicidal thoughts — **contact 988 (Suicide and Crisis Lifeline) immediately**
- [ ] Dorsal shutdown not responding to self-help, impacting work or relationships
- [ ] History of trauma surfacing
- [ ] Substance use increasing in response to stress

If you checked any of these — please make a plan to access professional support. This is outside the scope of self-help.

**Resources:**

- 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988)
- Your primary care physician (can refer or assess for medication)
- Psychology Today therapist directory (psychologytoday.com/us/therapists)
- BetterHelp / Talkspace for telehealth therapy

## Try this for 14 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**Three 5-question check-ins per day for two weeks. AM, midday, evening. Just observe — don't try to change anything.**

That's the baseline-establishment exercise. Most people are surprised by what they find.

After 14 days you'll have data on your actual nervous-system patterns. That data is the foundation for everything else.

Bring this workbook to your next session.
