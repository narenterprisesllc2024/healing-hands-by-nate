## Try this for 30 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**Daily 10-minute mobility practice + daily 20-minute walk + 8 hours of sleep. For 30 days.**

These three basics, done consistently for 30 days, often produce measurable functional improvement at any age.

If you're 40 and curious how good your body can feel — try this.

If you're 70 and worried about decline — try this.

The body responds to consistent attention regardless of starting point.

Bring this workbook to your next session.
