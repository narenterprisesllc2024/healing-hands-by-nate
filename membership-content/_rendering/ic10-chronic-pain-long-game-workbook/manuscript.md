# Chronic Pain — The Long Game — Companion Workbook

*A year-long companion to the IC10 deep cut. Build your multi-modal management plan.*

---

## How to use this workbook

Year-long reference. Self-assessment first. Build your team. Establish daily practice. Track over time. Flare plan ready when needed.

Bring to all healthcare appointments — having this written record helps every provider you work with.

## Try this for 30 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**Three basics. Every day. For 30 days.**

1. Sleep 8 hours with consistent schedule
2. 10 minutes of gentle daily movement
3. 5 minutes of slow exhale breathing before bed

Don't add anything else. Don't change anything else.

The three basics done consistently for 30 days often reduce chronic pain intensity by a meaningful amount in many people. Not always. Often.

If it helps — build from there. If it doesn't — make sure your team is right.

Bring this workbook to your next session.
