# The Vagus Reset — Companion Workbook

*A printable companion to the Month 4 module. Train your vagal tone in 30 days.*

---

## How to use this workbook

Print it. Put it where you'll do the daily practice — bathroom mirror, kitchen counter, desk.

Vagal tone training is gradual. You won't see dramatic day-1 changes. You will see cumulative shifts by week 3 if you do the daily practice consistently. The workbook keeps you honest.

Bring it to your next session.

## Try this for 7 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**10 slow exhale breaths every morning before getting out of bed. Inhale 4, exhale 8. For one week.**

Just 10 breaths. Lying down. Before your feet hit the floor.

Most people notice their default daytime stress level drops within a week. This is one of the most leveraged 90 seconds of your day.
