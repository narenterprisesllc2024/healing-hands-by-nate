## Safety Checklist (Read Before Doing Self-Massage)

Areas I will AVOID:

- [ ] Front and sides of neck
- [ ] Directly over major joints
- [ ] Directly on the spine
- [ ] Over kidneys (lower back under floating ribs)
- [ ] Behind the knee
- [ ] Inner upper arm and thigh (major arteries)
- [ ] Varicose vein areas
- [ ] Acute injuries (first 48-72 hours)

I will STOP immediately if:

- [ ] Sharp shooting pain (not deep-release discomfort)
- [ ] Numbness or tingling
- [ ] Dizziness or nausea
- [ ] Spreading bruising

I will NOT do self-massage during:

- [ ] Pregnancy (without OB clearance for specific areas)
- [ ] Active blood clots or DVT history
- [ ] Active infection or fever
- [ ] First 48-72 hours after acute injury
- [ ] Recent surgery (without medical clearance)

## Try this for 7 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**Buy the starter kit. Lacrosse ball, tennis ball, foam roller. Under $30 total. Use one tool, one area, for 5 minutes a day, every day, for one week.**

That's the experiment. Don't try to use everything. Pick the tool that matches what's bothering you most right now and just use that one.

Most people are surprised by how much benefit comes from one focused 5-minute daily practice. Once you're consistent with one, add another.

Bring this workbook to your next session.
