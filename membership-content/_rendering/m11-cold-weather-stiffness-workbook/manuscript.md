# Cold-Weather Stiffness — Companion Workbook

*A printable companion to the Month 11 module. Build the winter routine that keeps you mobile.*

---

## How to use this workbook

Print it. Put it on your nightstand or bathroom mirror. Fill it in over the next 7 days.

Winter is a four-month event. The bodies that come through it mobile are the ones whose owners built a small daily routine in November and stuck with it. This workbook helps you build that routine.

## Try this for 7 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**The in-bed warmup before you even sit up. Every morning. For one week.**

That's it. Three to four minutes. Lying down.

Ankle circles. Knees to chest. Spinal twist. Arms overhead. (Then neck rotations after you sit up.)

If you do nothing else and just do that, you'll feel a difference in how the first hour of your day goes by day 4-5.

Bring this workbook to your next session. We'll talk about what's working and where to push deeper.
