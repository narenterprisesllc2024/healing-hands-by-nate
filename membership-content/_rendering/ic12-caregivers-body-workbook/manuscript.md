## Try this for 30 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**Doorframe stretch every time you walk through a doorway. For 30 days.**

That's the practice. No dedicated time. Just every time you go through a door — 30 seconds of doorframe stretch.

If you walk through doorways 10 times a day, that's 5 minutes of cumulative chest/shoulder work without adding ANY time to your day.

After 30 days, most caregivers see noticeable reduction in upper-body tension.

If this works — build from there. If it doesn't — at least you tried something that didn't require finding time you don't have.

Bring this workbook to your next session. You deserve someone paying attention to your body.
