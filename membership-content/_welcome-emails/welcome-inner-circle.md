Most Inner Circle members benefit from starting with a **deep cut** — the one most relevant to your body or life situation right now:

- **The Year of Your Lower Back** — if chronic low back is your main issue
- **The Year of Your Neck and Shoulders** — if desk work has wrecked you

Pick the one that matches your current situation. Read it. Reference it. Use the companion workbook.
