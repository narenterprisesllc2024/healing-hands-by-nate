# The Stress-Body Connection — Companion Workbook

*A year-long companion to the IC7 deep cut. Map your personal stress storage pattern and build the body-side recovery practice.*

---

## How to use this workbook

Year-long reference. Use the storage map self-assessment first to baseline. Then the daily/weekly trackers for ongoing awareness. Bring relevant pages to therapy too if you're doing that work.

## Mental Health Support Check

Honest assessment — check anything that applies over the past month:

- [ ] Anxiety significantly impairing daily function
- [ ] Persistent depression lasting more than 2-3 weeks
- [ ] Panic attacks
- [ ] Trauma symptoms (intrusive memories, hypervigilance, emotional flashbacks)
- [ ] Substance use increasing as coping
- [ ] Suicidal thoughts — **contact 988 immediately**
- [ ] Stress patterns tied to specific past events you keep returning to
- [ ] Relationship dynamics you can't seem to shift on your own

If any of these are checked — body work alone isn't enough. Please access professional mental health support.

## Try this for 7 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**Three 60-second body scans per day for one week. AM, midday, evening. Just observe — don't try to change anything.**

That's the foundational practice. By day 7 you'll have your personal stress storage map.

Once you know your storage map, the rest of the work — daily tissue release, nervous system practices, bodywork, possibly therapy — has a clear target.

Bring this workbook to your next session.
