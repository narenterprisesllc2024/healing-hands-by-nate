# Seasonal Recovery — The Harvest — Companion Workbook

*A printable companion to the Month 10 module. Plan your recovery the way you planned your work.*

---

## How to use this workbook

Print it. Put it on your fridge or your bedroom wall — somewhere you'll see it every day for the next 6 weeks.

Fall recovery isn't a meditation exercise. It's planning. Just like you'd plan a season's work. This workbook helps you turn "I should rest more" into specific, trackable, daily commitments.

Bring it to your next session.

## Try this for 7 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**Bed half an hour earlier. Every night. For one week.**

Just that one thing. Don't add stretches yet. Don't drop tasks yet. Just sleep more.

Most people are running an hour of sleep debt minimum after a hard summer. Paying that down is the leverage point. Once the sleep starts restoring you, the other commitments get easier to make and keep.

Bring this workbook to your next session. We'll go over what changed and where to push next.
