# The Desk-Job Reset — Companion Workbook

*A printable companion to the Month 6 module. Fill it in as you go.*

---

## How to use this workbook

You don't have to use it perfectly. You don't have to do it every day. You don't have to "complete" it.

What you do have to do: actually look at your body and write down what you notice. Most of the people I work with don't know what their body is telling them because they've stopped listening. This workbook is a structured way to start listening again.

Print it out. Keep it on your desk. Fill it in over the next 7 days. Bring it to your next session.

## Part 3 — 7-Day Desk-Posture Tracker

For the next week, check in with yourself three times each workday. Mark where your body is right now.

### Daily prompts (use the same three for each day):

**Morning check (before work starts):**
- Energy level (1-10)
- Body tension areas (circle): neck / shoulders / mid-back / low back / hips / jaw
- One word for how your body feels

**Midday check (around lunch):**
- Did you do the 20-minute rule? (Y/N)
- Doorframe stretch done today? (Y/N)
- Body tension areas now (circle): neck / shoulders / mid-back / low back / hips / jaw

**End-of-day check:**
- Body tension areas now (circle): neck / shoulders / mid-back / low back / hips / jaw
- What hurts more than this morning?
- Did you do the 15-minute reset? (Y/N)

## Try this for 7 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**Doorframe stretch. Morning. 3 sets of 30 seconds. Every day for one week.**

That's it. That's the assignment. Don't over-engineer it. Don't make it a whole routine. Just stand in your doorframe in the morning before you start your day, lean through, breathe, and let your chest open.

Most clients report visible difference in how their body feels by day 4-5. If you don't feel anything different after a full week, message me and we'll figure out what's going on.

Bring this workbook to your next session. I want to see what you noticed.
