# The Year of Your Lower Back — Companion Workbook

*A year-long companion to the IC1 deep cut. Map your low back's patterns and track your progress.*

---

## How to use this workbook

This is a year-long workbook, not a 7-day tracker. Use the pattern self-assessment when something flares. Use the 30-day tracker when you start a new home practice. Use the seasonal check-ins at the start of each season.

Bring it to every session.

---

## Pattern Self-Assessment

Use this when low back pain shows up. Identify which pattern (or combination) you're dealing with.

### Right now, my low back feels:

**Where exactly?** (Mark on the diagram or describe)

- [ ] Center of low back, both sides
- [ ] One side only — [ ] Right / [ ] Left
- [ ] High low back, just under the ribs
- [ ] Low into the sacrum / SI joints
- [ ] Pain radiating down a leg (SEE DOCTOR)

**Pain level (0-10):** [____]

### Pattern questions

**1. When does it hurt most?** (Check all that apply)

- [ ] After sitting for a while → suggests Pattern 1 (Hip Flexor Pull)
- [ ] During lifting or carrying → suggests Pattern 2 (Glute Inhibition)
- [ ] When rotating, reaching behind, twisting → suggests Pattern 3 (Mid-Back Compensation)
- [ ] Started suddenly during specific activity → Pattern 4 (Acute Strain)
- [ ] On and off for 5+ years → Pattern 5 (Chronic/Recurrent)

**2. What makes it better?**

- [ ] Movement / walking
- [ ] Heat
- [ ] Hip flexor stretching
- [ ] Glute squeezes / bridges
- [ ] Opening the mid-back
- [ ] Nothing seems to help

**3. What makes it worse?**

- [ ] Sitting for long periods
- [ ] Lifting
- [ ] Standing too long
- [ ] Cold weather
- [ ] Stress
- [ ] Any movement at all (SEE DOCTOR IF SEVERE)

## Try this for 30 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**The daily maintenance routine (morning 3 min, midday 2 min, evening 5 min). Every day. For 30 days.**

That's 10 minutes a day total. Spread across three windows.

If your low back has been a problem for years and you actually do this for 30 days, you'll be in a different place by day 30. Most people are.

Bring this workbook to your next session — I want to see what your tracker shows.
