## Try this for 7 days (the on-ramp version)

If you can't commit to the full 30 days, start with this:

**5 minutes of stillness, before bed. Every night. For one week.**

Just sit or lie down. Eyes closed. 5 minutes. Don't do anything.

If you do nothing else from this workbook, do that. It's the smallest possible version of the reset. It still works.

Most people who start with just the 5-minute stillness end up adding the breathing and stretching naturally within a couple weeks because the stillness alone reveals how much they need the others.

Bring this workbook to your January session.
