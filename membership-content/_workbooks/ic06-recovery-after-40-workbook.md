## Try this for 30 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**A 15-minute daily practice. Same time. Same place. Every day for 30 days.**

That's the foundational ask. 15 minutes. Daily. Non-negotiable.

Most people in their 40s, 50s, 60s who actually do this for 30 days are stunned by the difference. The body responds to consistent attention at any age.

Bring this workbook to your next session.
