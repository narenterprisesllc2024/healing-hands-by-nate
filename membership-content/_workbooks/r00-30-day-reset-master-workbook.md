# The 30-Day Reset — Companion Workbook

*Daily tracker, weekly reflections, before-and-after body inventory. Use this to make the program stick.*

---

## How to use this workbook

Use it daily. Track what you did. Note what you felt. Compare beginning to end.

The data matters more than perfection. Even brief notes — one word, one number — adds up to a meaningful record of your 30 days.

Print it out if you can. Keep it where you'll do your practice. Bring it to your next session.
