# Movement Patterns for Daily Life — Companion Workbook

*A year-long companion to the IC9 deep cut. Systematically rebuild the fundamental movement patterns.*

---

## How to use this workbook

Year-long reference. Pattern self-assessment first. 30-day pattern practice tracker. Quarterly re-tests to see progress.

Bring it to every session.

---

## Pattern Self-Assessment Scorecard

Test each pattern and rate where you are honestly.

### Hinge Test

Stand. Feet hip-width apart. Slowly hinge forward from hips, knees slightly bent. Reach toward floor.

- [ ] Palms to floor (5)
- [ ] Fingertips to floor (4)
- [ ] Mid-shin (3)
- [ ] Knees only (2)
- [ ] Can't bend comfortably (1)

**Score: ___ / 5**

### Squat Test

Stand. Feet shoulder-width apart. Lower into squat as deep as possible, heels on floor.

- [ ] Below parallel easily (5)
- [ ] Parallel with effort (4)
- [ ] Partway, not parallel (3)
- [ ] Barely bend knees (2)
- [ ] Can't squat / heels lift (1)

**Score: ___ / 5**

### Get-Up Test

Sit on floor cross-legged. Stand up.

- [ ] Easy, no support (5)
- [ ] One hand for support (4)
- [ ] Two hands (3)
- [ ] Roll to knees first (2)
- [ ] Need significant help (1)

**Score: ___ / 5**

### Rotation Test

Stand. Cross arms over chest. Rotate without moving hips.

- [ ] See directly behind (5)
- [ ] Halfway behind (4)
- [ ] Just over shoulder (3)
- [ ] Barely past front (2)
- [ ] No rotation (1)

**Score: ___ / 5**

### Gait Quality

Walk past a window or have someone watch.

- [ ] Normal heel-to-toe roll, full hip extension, arm swing (5)
- [ ] Mostly normal but limited hip extension (4)
- [ ] Mostly flat foot, some shuffling (3)
- [ ] Significant shuffle (2)
- [ ] Limp or pain (1)

**Score: ___ / 5**

### Carry Test

Carry a moderately heavy bag 50 feet.

- [ ] Easy, no compensation (5)
- [ ] Some lean to opposite side (4)
- [ ] Significant lean or strain (3)
- [ ] Cannot do comfortably (2)
- [ ] Cannot do at all (1)

**Score: ___ / 5**

### TOTAL SCORE: ___ / 30

**Interpretation:**

- 25-30: Excellent pattern quality. Maintain with daily practice.
- 19-24: Good. Some areas to address. Daily practice will produce noticeable improvement.
- 12-18: Significant pattern dysfunction. Daily practice is critical. Consider PT or movement specialist.
- Below 12: Major mobility/strength issues. Professional support strongly recommended.

## Try this for 30 days

If you do nothing else from this workbook, try this:

**The get-up. Sit on the floor and stand up without using hands. Once a day. For 30 days.**

That's it. One get-up per day.

This single practice strengthens hip mobility, leg strength, core stability, and balance — the foundations of all the patterns. People who do this consistently see improvement across multiple patterns within 30 days.

Bring this workbook to your next session.
