# Membership Content: Year-in-a-Day Batch Record Script

This is your recording day runbook. Everything you need to sit down, press record, and knock out a full year of membership content in one focused day. No second-guessing. No "what was I going to say again." Just open the page, read your starting line, hit your bullets, say your closing line, move on.

---

## RECORDING DAY LOGISTICS

### Total estimated recording time

| Content block | Pieces | Avg length | Subtotal |
|---|---|---|---|
| 30-Day Reset daily prompts | 30 | 2-3 min | ~75 min |
| Monthly modules (Continuity) | 12 | 5-8 min | ~80 min |
| Monthly bonus protocols (Between Sessions) | 12 | 4-6 min | ~60 min |
| Quarterly PDF voice memos (Inner Circle) | 4 | 20-30 min | ~100 min |
| **Total raw recording** | **58 pieces** | | **~315 min (~5.25 hrs)** |

Add 30% for restarts, water, brain resets: **~7 hours wall-clock time.** That is a full day. Plan for it.

### Equipment needed

- Phone camera (vertical for video, horizontal if you prefer — pick one and stick with it all day)
- Decent light — face a window or set up one lamp at eye level. Don't overthink this.
- Quiet room. Tell the house. Turn off notifications. If the dog barks, restart the clip.
- A glass of water within arm's reach at all times
- This document open on a laptop or tablet where you can glance at it

### Energy management

- Work in 45-minute blocks. Record 5-8 pieces per block.
- 15-minute breaks between blocks. Walk outside. Don't look at your phone.
- Eat before you start. Have a real lunch midday, not a granola bar.
- Your voice and your energy will be best in the first 3 hours. That's when you do the video pieces. Voice memos for quarterly PDFs can happen when you're tired — they're just you talking to Sovi, not performing.
- If you feel yourself going flat, stop. Walk. Come back in 20 minutes. Flat energy shows up on camera and your people will feel it.

### Batch order

Record in this order. It's designed to build momentum with easy wins first.

1. **30-Day Reset daily prompts** (30 pieces, 2-3 min each) — shortest, simplest, fastest wins. Gets you warmed up and rolling.
2. **Monthly modules** (12 pieces, 5-8 min each) — the meat. You're in the groove by now.
3. **Monthly bonus protocols** (12 pieces, 4-6 min each) — targeted, practical, no theory.
4. **Quarterly PDF voice memos** (4 pieces, 20-30 min each) — just talking, no camera needed. End-of-day brain dump energy is fine here.

### If you don't finish

If you only get through half today, that's fine. The other half is next Saturday. The 30-Day Reset and six months of modules is a fully launchable product. Don't let "all or nothing" thinking kill a perfectly good recording day.
